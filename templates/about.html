How to play

Welcome to Triviamasters! Here's a short guide on how to play.

Singleplayer mode

Play a quick game of Trivia to test your knowlegde!

Multiplayer without an account

Play multiplayer without an account. Start a game and send the unique code to your opponent. After they've filled in the code, the game is a go!

Multiplayer with an account

In order to keep track of your scores and see yourself on a virtual leaderboard, you will have to register and login. Once on your personal homepage, you can see your current games, view the leaderboard tab and keep track of your friends. You can then invite your friends to game and find out who's the true Triviamaster!
